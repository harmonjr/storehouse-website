## Proposed Changes

### Change 1: Remove "Here's the real insight:" hedge phrase

**Priority:** Medium
**Why:** AI Tells (hedge phrase) and Craft Guide condescension radar. "Here's the real insight:" positions the writer as revealing something to the reader rather than walking alongside them. The insight is self-evident from the content that follows.

> **Current (line 83):**
> Here's the real insight: traditional budgets fail because they distribute discipline across every single purchase. Every trip to the store is a decision. Every category is a potential failure. The cognitive load is enormous, and eventually, everyone gives up.

> **Proposed:**
> Traditional budgets fail because they distribute discipline across every single purchase. Every trip to the store is a decision. Every category is a potential failure. The cognitive load is enormous, and eventually, everyone gives up.

Deleting four words. The paragraph is stronger without the announcement.

---

### Change 2: Vary the "even if it's $0 today" repetition

**Priority:** Medium
**Why:** AI Tells (symmetrical structure). The same parenthetical appears on consecutive lines 96 and 97 with identical phrasing. Varying one breaks the templated pattern without losing the permission-giving function.

> **Current (lines 96-97):**
> 2. Subtract what you give (even if it's $0 today -- be honest)
> 3. Subtract what you save (even if it's $0 today)

> **Proposed:**
> 2. Subtract what you give (even if it's $0 today -- be honest)
> 3. Subtract what you save (start where you are, not where you think you should be)

This variation echoes "Setting Your Hierarchy" (line 111: "Start where you are. Not where you think you should be"), creating a subtle cross-article connection while eliminating the mechanical repetition.
